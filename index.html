Rijkmuseum

The Rijksmuseum is the largest art museum in the Netherlands and has an impressive collection of world-famous works by masters such as Vincent van Gogh, Johannes Vermeer, Frans Hals and Rembrandt van Rijn. Using both works of art and historical objects, the National Museum of the Netherlands looks back on more than 800 years of Dutch history more info

Van Gogh Museum

The Van Gogh Museum is a Dutch art museum dedicated to the works of Vincent van Gogh and his contemporaries in the Museum Square in Amsterdam South, close to the Stedelijk Museum, the Rijksmuseum, and the Concertgebouw. The museum opened on 2 June 1973, and its buildings were designed by Gerrit Rietveld and Kisho Kurokawa. more info

Bike Tour

This bike tour gives you a fascinating picture of the famous and rich history of Amsterdam. During the two-hour bike ride you will come to interesting places, such as the harbor, the charming Jordaan, the Anne Frank house, the Westerkerk and the Rijksmuseum .The 3-hour tour adds places that often remain hidden. The undiscovered Amsterdam with its idyllic canals, quiet hidden courtyards and photogenic views. more info
